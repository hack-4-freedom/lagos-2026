# OwnStack

**Hack4Freedom Lagos 2026**

---

## Overview

OwnStack is a Bitcoin-native business infrastructure platform designed to help entrepreneurs and small business owners manage invoices, payments, and business records independently.

The platform enables users to create portable business identities, generate invoices, receive Lightning payments, and maintain ownership of important business records using freedom-focused technologies like Nostr and Bitcoin Lightning.

OwnStack focuses especially on underserved entrepreneurs operating through informal systems such as WhatsApp, Instagram, and manual bookkeeping.

---

## Problem

Millions of small business owners across Africa rely heavily on centralized platforms and informal systems to run their businesses.

This creates major problems:

* Lost invoices and receipts
* Poor payment tracking
* No portable business identity
* Dependence on centralized platforms
* Difficulty proving transactions
* Lack of ownership over business records

If accounts get restricted, devices are stolen, or chats disappear, critical business information can be lost.

---

## Solution

OwnStack provides a lightweight business infrastructure platform that helps entrepreneurs:

* Create business identities
* Generate invoices
* Track payments
* Receive Bitcoin Lightning payments
* Maintain portable business records

The platform combines modern fintech UX with freedom-tech principles such as self-sovereign identity and user-owned infrastructure.
